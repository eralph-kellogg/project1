Chocolate Chip Cookies

Kellogg-approved.
Time to Make: 1 hour
Skill Level

Ingredients

  • 2 C + 2 T all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 t baking soda
  • 1/2 t salt
  • 12 T butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 C packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 C granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + egg yolk
  • 2 t vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 C semi-sweet chocolate chips*

***Let's stop cattin' around and be honest... you should probably put the whole bag of chocolate chips in.

PS. The secret to the best chocolate cookies ever? The best chocolate chips.

Instructions

[Betty's Way]
  1. Preheat over to 325 deg F
  2. Melt together butter in the microwave and let it cool.
  3. Beat together butter + sugars.
  4. Mix in egg, yolk, and vanilla extract.
  5. Add in baking soda + salt.
  6. Carefully mix in flour.
  7. Add in chocolate chips.
  8. Scoop onto cookie sheet.
  9. Bake for 10-15 minutes, depending on oven temperatures and desired doneness.
