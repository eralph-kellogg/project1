Beer Bread

Time to Make: 1 hour
Skill Level

Ingredients

  • 3 C all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 C sugar
  • 1 T baking powder
  • 1 t salt
  • 1 bottle (12oz) of beer
  • 1 stick unsalted

Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 350 deg F
  2. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan.
  3. Whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Stir the beer into dry ingredients until just mixed.
  5. Pour half the melted butter into the bottom of the load pan.
  6. Pour batter into pan, then top with the remainder of the butter.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes, until goldren brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
