All About the Chef

Betty is a cool cat. Literally.

Born & adopted somewhere outside of Cleveland, Ohio, Betty now resides in Evanston, Illinois. Her favorite activities are napping, more napping, detroying houseplants, eating, playing with her sock kitty, and even more napping. She is the go-to taste tester for all food cooked in the apartment, whether the human realizes it or not.

Now that the human is home all day long, Betty has decided to help the human out and compile some of her favorite recipes.
