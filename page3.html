Sandwich Bread

Time to Make: 3 hours
Skill Level

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 t instant yeast (two 0.25oz packets)
  • 3/4 C + 2 2/3 C warm water (divided)
  • 1/4 C granulated sugar
  • 1 T salt
  • 3 T unsalted butter, cubed (room temp)
  • 9-10 C all-purpose flour
  • 3 T unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, stir to dissolve the yeast in 3/4 C of the warm water. Let sit for 5 minutes so yeast can proof.
  2. Add the remaining 2 2/3 C water, sugar, salt, room temperature butter, and 5 C of flour. Stir to combine.
  3. Carefully mix and add remaining flour until dough is soft and tacky (but not sticky).
  4. Place dough in a lightly greased bolw and turn it over so that it's completedly coated. Cover with plastic wrap and set in a draft-free place to rise until doubled in size (about 45 mins to 1 hour).
  5. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface. Press all over to remove any air pockets.
  6. Divide dough in two. Pat each into a 9x12-inch rectangle. Roll up the rectangles into tight cyclinders (starting on short end). Pinch to seal the seams and ends, and place each into a greased 9-inch loaf pan.
  7. Cover loaves loosely and let rise again, until doubled in size (30 to 45 minutes).
  8. Position oven rack on the lowest setting and preheat over to 400 deg F.
  9. Brush loaves with some melted butter and bake loaves for 30 to 35 minutes (rotating halfway through).
  10. Remove from oven and brush with more of the melted butter. Allow to cool for 10 minutes, then remove from the pans and cool completely before slicing.
